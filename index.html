ABOUT

FLOWING WAVE
OF DREAMS

BLANCHETT

For this project, I want to merge the rhythmic nature of brain waves, the progression of sleep stages, and the emotional tides of sleep into a fluid, dream-like visual representation.

Sleep is not a static state but a continuous movement through different depths, much like waves in the ocean. Each sleep stage corresponds to a unique type of brain wave, and these oscillations influence our emotions, memories, and subconscious experiences. The color gradients in my work reflect this dynamic process, showing a gradual immersion into deeper sleep before rising again in REM.

Each night, I drift between light ripples, deep currents, and stormy dream waves, gradually restoring my emotional balance.

Is your dream like a calm tide or crashing waves? Tell me, where does your dream begin?
